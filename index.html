About Me

As a licensed drone pilot with expertise in software development, I bring precision and thoroughness to every project. I understand the unique needs of builders and homeowners, and using AI, I can create detailed 3D models of the project that allow you to be in the driver's seat of whatever you want to more specifically inspect.

About Dracula Drones

Founded in 2024, Dracula Drones LLC specializes in creating detailed 3D scans and detailed walkthrough videos of construction projects. Our mission is to provide builders and clients with accurate, comprehensive progress tracking that helps keep projects on schedule and within budget.

Whether you need detailed roof inspections, pre-construction site scouting, or ongoing build documentation, our aerial and 360 degree perspectives reveals crucial details that ground-level inspections might miss.

  • Regular progress documentation
  • Highly interactible 3D scans
  • Detailed roof inspection footage
  • Pre-construction site scouting
  • Easy online access to project updates

Equipment

DJI Mini 2

Our lightweight mapping specialist. Despite its compact size, this drone captures high-quality 4K footage and 12MP stills perfect for construction documentation and roof inspections. Its sub-250g weight and agile control make it incredibly versatile for quick site surveys and detailed roofing assessments.

DJI Avata 2

Our FPV powerhouse. The immersive flying experience and stabilized 4K camera allow us to capture unique perspectives and detailed close-up inspections of construction elements. Perfect for dynamic site exploration and detailed structural documentation.

Insta360 ONE X2

Our ground-level documentation specialist. This 360-degree camera captures immersive walkthroughs and detailed interior progress. Perfect for comprehensive room-by-room documentation and creating virtual tour videos of completed spaces.
